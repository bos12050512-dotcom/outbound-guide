❓ Controller FAQ

"Does Outbound support controllers?"

Yes! Outbound is Steam Verified and fully supports Xbox and PlayStation controllers out of the box.

"Can I use a cheap third-party controller?"

You can, but we recommend official controllers. Third-party controllers often have input lag and compatibility issues.

"Wireless or Wired?"

Wireless is better for cozy gaming on your couch. Xbox controllers have 40hr battery life, more than enough for long sessions.
