Outbound Van Upgrades & Layout Guide

Your van is your base. Plan space, prioritize the right modules, and avoid expensive teardowns.

[ Ad slot - top banner ]

Module Priority (recommended)

  1. Storage Module S — first upgrade, always. More loot per trip.
  2. Basic Workbench — unlocks all other crafting.
  3. Cooking Station — converts raw food, extends food shelf life.
  4. Sleeping Quarters — required for time-skip / save in many survival games.
  5. Solar Panel + Battery — power stability before adding more modules.
  6. Planter Box — long-term renewable food.
  7. Storage Module L — late-game expansion.
  8. Refrigeration Unit — only after stable energy.

Common Layout Mistakes

  • Putting workstations far apart — wastes time walking.
  • Building decoration before utility — looks great, slows progression.
  • Stacking heat sources (cooking + fridge nearby) — possible efficiency penalty (pending verification).
  • No reserved space for late-game modules — forced teardown later.
