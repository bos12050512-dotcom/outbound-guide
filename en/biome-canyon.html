Canyon Biome Guide峡谷区域指南

A harsh, arid landscape that tests your survival skills. Manage engine overheating, discover rare resources, and uncover hidden collectibles in the desert.一片考验生存技能的严酷干旱景观。管理引擎过热、发现稀有资源，并在沙漠中揭开隐藏的收集品。

🏜️ Second Biome🏜️ 第二区域

📍 Overview📍 概览

The Canyon is the second biome in Outbound and a significant step up in difficulty. This dry, arid landscape introduces new dangers — most notably, your van's engine can overheat during the scorching daytime, forcing you to seek shade or wait until nightfall. Resources here are harder to find and often require better tools. However, the Canyon also holds some of the rarest materials in the game, making it essential for mid-game progression.峡谷是 Outbound 的第二个区域，难度显著提升。这片干燥的干旱景观引入了新的危险——最值得注意的是，你的房车引擎在炎热的白天可能过热，迫使你寻找遮蔽处或等到夜晚。这里的资源更难找到，通常需要更好的工具。然而，峡谷也拥有游戏中最稀有的材料之一，对中期进度至关重要。

🗺️ Landmarks🗺️ 地标

🌉 Canyon Bridge峡谷大桥

A massive natural rock bridge spanning the deepest part of the canyon. Cross it for a shortcut to the eastern section. There's a hidden path underneath — look for a narrow trail along the canyon wall.一座横跨峡谷最深处的巨大天然石桥。穿过它可以快速到达东部区域。桥下有一条隐藏路径——沿着峡谷壁寻找一条狭窄的小路。

🏛️ Ancient Ruins古代遗迹

Mysterious stone structures in the canyon's deepest reaches. Contain lore tablets, rare loot, and a hidden collectible or two. Some areas require climbing gear to access.位于峡谷最深处的神秘石质建筑。包含传说石板、稀有战利品和一两个隐藏收集品。某些区域需要攀爬装备才能进入。

🧝 Hidden Gnome Spots隐藏矮人位置

Several gnomes are tucked away in tricky canyon locations — behind waterfalls, under rock overhangs, and on narrow cliff ledges. Be aware of the known Gnome Bug that can make some gnomes not appear.几个矮人隐藏在棘手的峡谷位置——瀑布后面、岩石悬垂下方和狭窄的悬崖壁架上。请注意已知的矮人Bug，可能导致某些矮人无法出现。

📡 Canyon Signal Tower峡谷信号塔

Located on a high plateau accessible via a winding road. Requires Scrap Metal and Copper Wire to repair. Activating it reveals desert landmarks and the path to the Coast biome.位于通过蜿蜒道路可到达的高原上。需要废金属和铜线来修复。激活它会显示沙漠地标和通往海岸区域的路径。

🌡️ Survival Tips🌡️ 生存建议

🚨 Engine Overheating at Night🚨 夜间引擎过热

Yes, your engine can overheat at night in the Canyon — a commonly reported issue in Steam discussions. The desert retains heat long after sunset. Tip: Keep your van parked in shaded canyon overhangs during the day to reduce heat buildup. Upgrade your engine cooling system before entering this biome if possible.是的，在峡谷中你的引擎在夜间也会过热——这是Steam讨论中常见的问题报告。沙漠在日落后很长时间内仍保持热量。提示：白天将房车停在有遮蔽的峡谷悬垂下方以减少热量积累。如果可能，在进入此区域之前升级引擎冷却系统。

💧 Water Management💧 水资源管理

Water is scarce in the Canyon. Stock up before leaving the Grasslands. Oasis locations are your primary refill points. Build water storage modules in your van for extended exploration trips.峡谷中水资源稀缺。离开草原前要储备充足。绿洲是你主要的补给点。在房车中建造储水模块以延长探索旅程。

☀️ Shade for Energy Panels☀️ 能量面板遮阳

Counter-intuitively, extreme heat can reduce solar panel efficiency. Place your van in partial shade when parked for maximum energy generation. Solar Crystals can boost output during peak hours.反直觉的是，极端高温会降低太阳能板的效率。停车时将房车放在半阴处以获得最大的能量产出。太阳能水晶可以在高峰时段提升产出。

🎣 Fishing Spots🎣 钓鱼点

Fishing in the Canyon is limited to specific oasis locations and the canyon river. The fish here are adapted to warmer waters and tend to be rarer than Grasslands catches.峡谷中钓鱼仅限于特定的绿洲地点和峡谷河流。这里的鱼类适应了温暖水域，通常比草原的鱼类更稀有。

Hidden Oasis Pool隐藏绿洲水池

A small, secluded pool fed by underground springs. Best spot for Desert Catfish.由地下泉水补给的小型隐蔽水池。钓沙漠鲶鱼的最佳地点。

Common: Desert Catfish | Uncommon: Golden Carp普通：沙漠鲶鱼 | 稀有：金鲤

Canyon River Bend峡谷河湾

The main river flowing through the canyon floor. Good variety of warm-water fish.流经峡谷底部的主要河流。有各种暖水鱼类。

Common: Golden Carp | Uncommon: Desert Catfish普通：金鲤 | 稀有：沙漠鲶鱼

🏆 Collectibles🏆 收集品

🧝
Garden Gnomes花园矮人

4-5 gnomes in tricky canyon locations. Watch for the Gnome Bug — some may not spawn until you reload the area.4-5个矮人在棘手的峡谷位置。注意矮人Bug——有些可能需要重新加载区域才会出现。

🪨
Cairns石堆

3-4 cairns placed on narrow cliff edges and hidden paths. Some require careful platforming to reach.3-4个石堆在狭窄的悬崖边缘和隐藏路径上。有些需要精心的平台跳跃才能到达。

📜
Lore Tablets传说石板

Ancient stone tablets found near the ruins. Provide backstory and world-building details.在遗迹附近发现的古代石板。提供背景故事和世界构建细节。

➡️ Ready for the Coast?➡️ 准备好前往海岸了吗？

After surviving the Canyon's harsh desert, the Coast biome offers a refreshing change. Beaches, ocean fishing, and the ferry to the final area await.在峡谷严酷的沙漠中幸存下来后，海岸区域提供了一个清新的转变。海滩、海洋钓鱼和通往最终区域的渡轮在等着你。
