The final frontier. Snow-capped peaks, the rarest resources, and endgame content await at the top of the world. Complete your journey with the hot air balloon and conquer the summit.最后的边境。雪顶山峰、最稀有的资源和终局内容在世界之巅等着你。乘坐热气球完成你的旅程并征服顶峰。

📍 Overview📍 概览

The Mountain is the fourth and final biome in Outbound. It represents the ultimate challenge — freezing temperatures, steep terrain, and the rarest resources in the game. Snow blankets every surface, and visibility can drop during blizzards. This biome contains endgame content including the Mountain Peak (summit climb), a Hot Air Balloon station, developer Easter eggs, and the final Signal Tower. Only the most prepared players should attempt to explore this area fully. Make sure you've upgraded your van's insulation and heating before arriving.山地区域是 Outbound 的第四个也是最后一个区域。它代表了终极挑战——冰冻温度、陡峭地形和游戏中最稀有的资源。积雪覆盖每一个表面，暴风雪期间能见度会下降。这个区域包含终局内容，包括山峰（登顶）、热气球站、开发者彩蛋和最终信号塔。只有准备最充分的玩家才应该尝试全面探索这个区域。确保在到达之前已升级房车的隔热和供暖系统。

🗺️ Landmarks🗺️ 地标

🏔️ Mountain Peak山峰

The highest point in the game. Reaching the summit unlocks the "Climb the Mountain" achievement. The path is treacherous — narrow ledges, ice patches, and low visibility. Bring warm clothing, extra food, and fully upgraded climbing gear. The view from the top is breathtaking and reveals the entire game map.游戏中的最高点。到达山顶会解锁"攀登高山"成就。路径险恶——狭窄的壁架、冰面和低能见度。带上保暖衣物、额外的食物和完全升级的攀爬装备。从顶部俯瞰的景色令人叹为观止，可以揭示整个游戏地图。

🎈 Hot Air Balloon Station热气球站

A launching pad for a hot air balloon near the mountain's base. Riding the balloon unlocks the "Fly to the Sky" achievement and provides an aerial tour of the entire Mountain biome. Requires fuel — gather enough before attempting. The balloon ride is also one of the most memorable experiences in the game.山地基地附近的热气球发射场。乘坐热气球会解锁"飞向天空"成就，并提供整个山地区域的空中游览。需要燃料——在尝试之前收集足够的燃料。热气球之旅也是游戏中最令人难忘的体验之一。

👾 Developer Easter Eggs开发者彩蛋

Hidden throughout the Mountain are special locations that unlock the "Meet the Dev Team" achievement. These are carefully placed developer messages, team member names etched into stone, and secret rooms. Finding all of them rewards a unique cosmetic item. Check the community wiki for the latest locations.山地中隐藏着特殊的位置，可以解锁"与开发团队会面"成就。这些是精心放置的开发者留言、刻在石头上的团队成员名字和秘密房间。找到所有这些会奖励一个独特的装饰物品。请查看社区Wiki了解最新位置。

📡 Final Signal Tower最终信号塔

The last Signal Tower in the game, located near the summit. Repairing and activating it completes the signal network and reveals all remaining map markers. Requires Titanium and Rare Earth Magnets — plan your resource gathering accordingly. This is a key milestone for 100% completion.游戏中最后一个信号塔，位于顶峰附近。修复并激活它完成信号网络并显示所有剩余的地图标记。需要钛和稀土磁铁——相应地规划你的资源收集。这是100%完成度的关键里程碑。

🎣 Fishing Spots🎣 钓鱼点

Fishing in the Mountain is limited to frozen lakes and mountain streams. The fish here are adapted to icy waters and include some of the rarest catches in the game.山地中钓鱼仅限于冰冻湖泊和山地溪流。这里的鱼类适应了冰冷的水域，包括游戏中一些最稀有的捕获物。

Frozen Lake (Base Camp)冰冻湖（大本营）

The largest body of water in the Mountain biome. Fish through holes in the ice.山地区域最大的水体。通过冰洞钓鱼。

Common: Arctic Char | Uncommon: Ice Fish普通：北极红点鲑 | 稀有：冰鱼

Summit Stream峰顶溪流

A fast-flowing stream near the peak. Small window for fishing before it freezes over at night.靠近顶峰的快速流动溪流。夜间结冰前的小窗口钓鱼时间。

Common: Ice Fish | Uncommon: Arctic Char普通：冰鱼 | 稀有：北极红点鲑

Glacial Cavern Pool冰川洞穴水池

⚠️ Hidden cave pool accessible only through a crevasse. Home to the Legendary Glacier Sturgeon!⚠️ 仅通过裂缝进入的隐藏洞穴水池。传奇冰川鲟鱼的家园！

Rare: Arctic Char | LEGENDARY: Glacier Sturgeon稀有：北极红点鲑 | 传奇：冰川鲟鱼
