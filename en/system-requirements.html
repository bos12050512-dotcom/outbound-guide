Outbound System Requirements

Official minimum and recommended PC specs from the Steam store page, plus practical FPS expectations for popular GPUs.

[ Ad slot - top banner ]

Minimum

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • CPU: Intel Core i3-10100F / AMD Ryzen 3 3100
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1050 / AMD RX 570
  • DirectX: 11
  • Storage: 16 GB

Recommended

  • OS: Windows 11 64-bit
  • CPU: Intel i3-12100F / Ryzen 7 1700 or better
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • GPU: NVIDIA RTX 3060 / AMD RX 7600 XT or better
  • DirectX: 12
  • Storage: 16 GB

Can my PC run Outbound?

  • Old laptop with integrated graphics: probably not. The minimum GPU is GTX 1050.
  • Office PC with GTX 1050 / RX 570: yes, on low settings, 1080p, 30–45 FPS.
  • Mid-range gaming PC (1660 / 3060): comfortable on high settings 1080p.
  • 4K gaming: not officially documented. Recommend 4070+ for stable 4K.
