Why Layout Matters布局为何重要

Your camper van is more than a vehicle — it's your mobile base. In Outbound, the interior layout directly affects three critical systems: weight distribution (unbalanced vans handle poorly), power grid efficiency (proximity matters for some energy connections), and accessibility (can you reach everything you need without climbing over furniture?). 你的露营车不仅仅是一辆载具 — 它是你的移动基地。在 Outbound 中，内饰布局直接影响三个关键系统：重量分配（不平衡的房车操控性差）、电力效率（距离对某些能源连接有影响）和可达性（你能否不翻越家具就能拿到所需物品？）。

A well-designed layout saves time, reduces power waste, and makes long expeditions far more comfortable. Planning before you build is always cheaper than dismantling and rebuilding. 精心设计的布局能节省时间、减少电力浪费，让长途远征更加舒适。先规划后建造总是比拆了重建更划算。

Layout Principles布局原则

1

Group Workstations Together将工作台分组

Place crafting stations, storage, and processing modules near each other. This creates efficient workflows — grab materials, process, craft, store — without running across the van. A "crafting corner" with all workstations within arm's reach is ideal.将合成站、存储和处理模块放在一起。这创造了高效的工作流 — 拿材料、处理、合成、存储 — 不用跑遍整个房车。一个所有工作台触手可及的"合成角"是最理想的。

2

Balance Weight on Both Sides两侧平衡重量

Heavy modules like batteries and storage chests should be distributed evenly across the van's left and right sides. Unbalanced weight makes driving less stable, especially off-road. Check your van's weight indicator after each major build.电池和存储箱等重型模块应在房车左右两侧均匀分布。不平衡的重量会导致驾驶不稳，尤其在越野时。每次重大建造后检查房车的重量指示器。

3

Leave Walking Space留出行走空间

It's tempting to fill every slot, but leave at least one clear aisle. You'll need to move between modules quickly during crafting, and a cramped van makes it easy to get stuck or accidentally interact with the wrong object.填满每个格子很诱人，但至少留一条清晰的通道。合成时你需要快速在模块间移动，拥挤的房车容易卡住或误触错误物体。

4

Balance Roof vs Interior平衡屋顶与内部

Roof space is premium real estate for solar panels and storage, but too many roof items increase wind resistance and reduce fuel efficiency. Mix roof modules (solar, antenna) with interior modules (crafting, storage) for the best balance.屋顶空间是太阳能板和存储的黄金地段，但过多屋顶物品会增加风阻、降低燃油效率。将屋顶模块（太阳能、天线）与内部模块（合成、存储）混合搭配以达到最佳平衡。

5

Plan for Expansion为扩展留余地

Don't fill every available slot early on. Leave 2-3 empty spots for late-game modules you haven't unlocked yet. Dismantling to make room later wastes resources and time.不要在早期填满每个可用槽位。为尚未解锁的后期模块留 2-3 个空位。之后拆了腾地方既浪费资源又浪费时间。

Popular Layout Concepts热门布局方案

The Compact Explorer紧凑探险型

Best for: Solo players who prioritize exploration适合：优先探索的单人玩家

This layout maximizes storage and mobility while keeping the interior minimal. You spend less time in the van and more time exploring biomes.此布局最大化存储和机动性，同时保持内饰简洁。你在房车里花更少时间，更多时间探索生态群系。

Interior:内部： Crafting bench, 1 storage, bed, small fridge合成台、1个存储、床、小冰箱
Roof:屋顶： Solar panel, antenna, storage box太阳能板、天线、存储箱
Power:电力： Single solar + 1 battery (lightweight)单太阳能 + 1电池（轻量）
Style:风格： Functional, minimal decoration功能为主，极少装饰
🏡

The Mobile Home移动之家型

Best for: Cozy players who want a decorated, livable space适合：想要装饰性、宜居空间的休闲玩家

Prioritize comfort and aesthetics. Fill the van with furniture, decorations, and cozy items. Perfect for players who enjoy the "van life" aspect of the game.优先考虑舒适性和美观。用家具、装饰和温馨物品填满房车。非常适合享受游戏"房车生活"层面的玩家。

Interior:内部： Full kitchen, bed, rugs, lamps, shelves, plants完整厨房、床、地毯、灯具、置物架、植物
Roof:屋顶： Solar panels, flower boxes, light strips太阳能板、花盆、灯带
Power:电力： Dual solar + 2 batteries (for lights)双太阳能 + 2电池（供电灯）
Style:风格： Max decoration, cozy atmosphere最大化装饰，温馨氛围
⚡

The Power Station发电站型

Best for: Advanced players with maxed-out crafting needs适合：有满级合成需求的高级玩家

Dedicated to maximum energy output and automation. Run multiple workstations simultaneously with a robust power grid. Ideal for endgame crafting and processing.专注于最大化能源输出和自动化。用强大的电力网格同时运行多个工作台。适合后期合成和处理。

Interior:内部： All workstations, multiple batteries, storage全部工作台、多个电池、存储
Roof:屋顶： Max solar panels, wind turbine, antenna最大太阳能板、风力涡轮、天线
Power:电力： Triple solar + wind + 3+ batteries三太阳能 + 风力 + 3+电池
Style:风格： Industrial, function over form工业风，功能优先

New Build Items in Patch 1.1.41.1.4 补丁新建造物品

Patch 1.1.4 introduced two highly requested building items that significantly improve van layout options: 1.1.4 补丁引入了两个备受期待的建造物品，显著改善了房车布局选择：

🪜 Stairs🪜 楼梯

Build stairs to access roof areas and elevated platforms. Previously, players had to jump or use improvised ramp structures. Stairs make multi-level van layouts practical and much cleaner.建造楼梯可到达屋顶区域和高台。之前玩家必须跳跃或使用临时斜坡结构。楼梯让多层房车布局变得实用且更整洁。

🚪 Doors🚪 门

Place doors to create separate rooms inside your van. This is a game-changer for organization — section off a "kitchen," "bedroom," or "workshop" area for better visual and functional separation.放置门可以在房车内创建独立房间。这对组织管理是颠覆性的改变 — 划分出"厨房"、"卧室"或"工坊"区域，实现更好的视觉和功能分离。

Practical Tips实用技巧

  • Tip: Test drive after every major change. Place a module, then take the van for a short drive to check handling. This catches weight issues early.每次重大改动后试驾。放置模块后，短距离驾驶检查操控性。这能及早发现重量问题。
  • Tip: Use the van's weight indicator. It shows left/right balance. Aim for the needle to stay centered.使用房车的重量指示器。它会显示左右平衡情况。目标是让指针保持在中间。
  • Tip: Screenshot your layout. Before major rebuilds, take a screenshot so you can reference or recreate your old setup if the new one doesn't work out.截图你的布局。重大重建前截图，如果新布局不理想可以参考或恢复旧布局。
  • Tip: In co-op, coordinate with your partner. Assign each player a "zone" to avoid cluttering the same areas.联机时与搭档协调。给每位玩家分配一个"区域"，避免在同一区域堆放物品。
