Grasslands Biome Guide草原区域指南

Your first steps in Outbound. A gentle introduction with basic resources, landmarks, and everything you need to get started.你在 Outbound 的第一步。温和的入门体验，包含基础资源、地标和所需的一切。

🌿 Starting Biome🌿 初始区域

📍 Overview📍 概览

The Grasslands is the first biome you encounter in Outbound. It serves as the game's tutorial area, offering a gentle introduction to core mechanics like resource gathering, crafting, and driving your van. The landscape features rolling green hills, scattered trees, calm ponds, and a few key landmarks that teach you how navigation and exploration work. This biome is designed to be forgiving — enemies (if any) are minimal, resources are plentiful, and the day/night cycle is mild.草原是你在 Outbound 中遇到的第一个区域。它作为游戏的教程区域，温和地介绍了资源采集、制作和驾驶房车等核心机制。景观包括连绵的绿色山丘、散落的树木、平静的池塘以及几个教你导航和探索的关键地标。这个区域的设计非常宽容——敌人（如果有的话）很少，资源丰富，昼夜循环温和。

⛏️ Key Resources⛏️ 主要资源

🪵
Wood木材

The most common resource. Gathered from trees and fallen logs.最常见的资源。从树木和倒伏的原木中采集。

Used for: Van repairs, building foundations, crafting tables用途：房车修理、建筑地基、制作工作台

🌿
Plant Fibers植物纤维

Found in bushes and tall grass. Renewable and easy to harvest.在灌木和高草中找到。可再生且易于采集。

Used for: Rope, basic clothing, crafting components用途：绳索、基础衣物、制作组件

🪨
Stone石头

Scattered across the biome. Break larger rocks with a pickaxe.散布在整个区域。用镐敲碎大石头获取。

Used for: Stone tools, furnace fuel, building walls用途：石器、炉子燃料、建筑墙壁

🔌
Copper Wire铜线

Uncommon resource found near rocky outcrops and old structures.在岩石露头和旧建筑附近发现的稀有资源。

Used for: Electrical wiring, energy modules, signal repairs用途：电线连接、能量模块、信号修复

🔧
Basic Plastics基础塑料

Salvaged from abandoned crates and roadside debris.从废弃箱子和路边碎片中回收获得。

Used for: Storage containers, van interior parts, water bottles用途：储物容器、房车内装零件、水瓶

🍄
Mushrooms蘑菇

Grow in shady, damp areas near trees. Some are edible, some are crafting materials.生长在树附近的阴凉潮湿区域。有些可食用，有些是制作材料。

Used for: Cooking, medicine, early crafting recipes用途：烹饪、药品、早期制作配方

🗺️ Landmarks to Visit🗺️ 必访地标

🔥 Fire Watchtower瞭望塔

One of the first major landmarks you'll see. Climb to the top for a panoramic view of the entire Grasslands. Interacting with it unlocks a story-related achievement. Great for getting your bearings and planning exploration routes.你将看到的第一个主要地标之一。爬到顶部可以俯瞰整个草原。互动后会解锁一个故事相关成就。非常适合了解方位和规划探索路线。

🏠 First Gnome Locations初始矮人位置

The Grasslands contain several Garden Gnomes for the collectible achievement. Check near the watchtower base, behind large boulders, and along the edges of forests. Refer to our Collectibles Guide for exact locations.草原包含多个用于收集成就的花园矮人。检查瞭望塔底部、大石头后面以及森林边缘。请参阅我们的收集品指南获取确切位置。

📡 Starting Signal Tower初始信号塔

Your first Signal Tower is located in the southern part of the Grasslands. Activating it reveals nearby points of interest on your map and is part of the core progression. Requires basic Copper Wire to repair — make sure you've gathered some before heading there.你的第一个信号塔位于草原南部。激活它会在地图上显示附近的兴趣点，是核心进度的一部分。需要基础铜线来修复——确保在前往之前已经收集了一些。

🐾 Paws & Whiskers Lodge爪子与胡须旅馆

Home to the pet adoption system. Visit here to adopt your first companion. Pets can assist with gathering resources, alerting you to nearby items, and providing cozy company on the road. Check our Pets Guide for available companions.宠物领养系统的所在地。来这里领养你的第一个伙伴。宠物可以帮助收集资源、提醒你附近有物品，并在路上提供温馨的陪伴。请查看我们的宠物指南了解可用的伙伴。

🎣 Fishing Spots🎣 钓鱼点

Fishing was expanded in Patch 1.1.4, adding new water bodies and fish types to the Grasslands. Here are the best early-game fishing locations:钓鱼功能在 1.1.4 补丁中进行了扩展，为草原增加了新的水体和鱼类。以下是最佳的前期钓鱼地点：

Mirror Lake镜湖

Calm, shallow lake near the starting area. Best for Sunfish and Bluegill.靠近起始区域的平静浅湖。最适合钓太阳鱼和蓝鳃鱼。

Common: Sunfish, Bluegill | Uncommon: Minnow普通：太阳鱼、蓝鳃鱼 | 稀有：小白条

Willow Creek柳溪

A narrow stream flowing through the eastern woods. Good for Minnows.一条穿过东部树林的狭窄溪流。适合钓小白条。

Common: Minnow | Uncommon: Bluegill普通：小白条 | 稀有：蓝鳃鱼

Frog Pond青蛙池塘

Small pond near Paws & Whiskers Lodge. Great for first-time fishing.爪子与胡须旅馆附近的小池塘。非常适合第一次钓鱼。

Common: Sunfish | Uncommon: Minnow普通：太阳鱼 | 稀有：小白条

Old Mill Reservoir旧磨坊水库

Deeper water near the western edge. Higher chance for uncommon catches.靠近西部边缘的深水区。稀有鱼类的几率更高。

Common: Bluegill | Uncommon: Sunfish, Minnow普通：蓝鳃鱼 | 稀有：太阳鱼、小白条

🏆 Collectibles🏆 收集品

🧝
Garden Gnomes花园矮人

5-6 gnomes hidden throughout the Grasslands. Check near landmarks and forest edges.草原中隐藏着5-6个矮人。检查地标附近和森林边缘。

🪨
Cairns石堆

Stacked rock formations marking hidden paths. 3-4 cairns in this biome.标记隐藏路径的堆石结构。此区域有3-4个石堆。

🧢
Bottle Caps瓶盖

Scattered near abandoned campsites and along roads. Collect for rewards.散落在废弃营地附近和道路两旁。收集可获奖励。

💡 Tips for Beginners💡 新手建议

  1. Explore before you build. Drive around the entire Grasslands first to unlock map markers and locate resource clusters. This saves time and prevents wasted trips later.先探索再建设。先驾车绕整个草原一圈，解锁地图标记并找到资源聚集点。这能节省时间并避免后续的白跑。
  2. Stockpile Wood and Stone early. These two resources are used in almost every early crafting recipe. Gather at least 50 of each before moving on.及早囤积木材和石头。几乎所有前期制作配方都会用到这两种资源。在继续之前至少各收集50个。
  3. Repair the Signal Tower first. Activating the Signal Tower reveals the entire biome on your map, making future exploration much more efficient.先修复信号塔。激活信号塔会在地图上显示整个区域，使未来的探索更加高效。
  4. Adopt a pet early. Pets at Paws & Whiskers Lodge can help you spot resources you might miss. They're more useful than they seem.尽早领养宠物。爪子与胡须旅馆的宠物可以帮助你发现可能遗漏的资源。它们比你想象的更有用。
  5. Don't rush to the next biome. The Grasslands has plenty to offer. Complete the collectibles and fishing here before moving on — some items are much harder to find later.不要急于前往下一个区域。草原有很多值得探索的内容。在继续之前，完成这里的收集品和钓鱼——有些物品后面会更难找到。

➡️ Ready for the Next Biome?➡️ 准备好进入下一个区域了吗？

Once you've explored the Grasslands, the Canyon awaits. Be prepared for harsher conditions, engine overheating, and rarer resources.一旦你探索完草原，峡谷就在前方。准备好面对更恶劣的条件、引擎过热和更稀有的资源。
