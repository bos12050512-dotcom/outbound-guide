Overview概述

Outbound lets you adopt animal companions to accompany you on your camper-van journey. Pets add warmth and personality to the game's cozy survival experience. You can find the Paws & Whiskers Lodge in the game world — it's your go-to location for adopting dogs, learning about animal care, and unlocking pet-related features. Outbound 允许你领养动物同伴来陪伴你的露营车旅程。宠物为游戏的温馨生存体验增添了温暖和个性。你可以在游戏世界中找到Paws & Whiskers Lodge（爪子与胡须旅馆） — 这是领养狗狗、了解动物护理和解锁宠物相关功能的首选地点。

Once adopted, your pet travels with you in the van, reacts to your actions, and can be fed, petted, and trained over time. Caring for your companion unlocks achievements and enriches the overall gameplay loop. 领养后，你的宠物会和你一起乘坐房车旅行、对你的行为做出反应，并且可以随着时间的推移进行喂养、抚摸和训练。照顾你的同伴可以解锁成就并丰富整体游戏体验。

How to Get a Pet如何获得宠物

🗺️

1. Find the Lodge1. 找到旅馆

Explore the game world to locate Paws & Whiskers Lodge. It's marked on your map once you discover it or unlock the nearby signal tower.探索游戏世界找到 Paws & Whiskers Lodge。发现它或解锁附近信号塔后，它会在地图上标注。

🤝

2. Interact & Adopt2. 互动与领养

Enter the lodge and interact with the adoption NPC. Browse available animals and choose your companion. Some may require completing a small quest first.进入旅馆并与领养NPC互动。浏览可用的动物并选择你的同伴。有些可能需要先完成一个小任务。

🚐

3. Welcome Home3. 欢迎回家

Your new pet will follow you to the van and settle in. Now you can start feeding, petting, and building your bond.你的新宠物会跟着你回到房车安顿下来。现在你可以开始喂养、抚摸并建立你们的羁绊。

Caring for Your Pet照顾你的宠物

🍖

Feeding喂食

Keep your pet fed with appropriate food items. Hungry pets may become less active or show visual cues that they need attention. Stock up on pet food before long trips.用合适的食物喂你的宠物。饥饿的宠物可能会变得不太活跃，或通过视觉提示表示需要关注。长途旅行前储备宠物食品。

🤗

Petting抚摸

Interact with your pet to pet them. Regular petting strengthens your bond and is required for the "Can I Pet the Dog?" achievement. Your companion responds with happy animations.与你的宠物互动来抚摸它们。定期抚摸可以增强你们的羁绊，也是"我可以摸狗吗？"成就的要求。你的同伴会用开心的动画回应。

🎓

Training训练

Over time, you can train your pet to perform actions or behaviors. Training mechanics may unlock as you progress, rewarding consistent interaction with your companion.随着时间推移，你可以训练宠物执行动作或行为。训练机制可能在推进过程中解锁，奖励持续与同伴互动的玩家。
