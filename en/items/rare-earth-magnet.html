How to get Rare Earth Magnet in Outbound

Rare Earth Magnets are essential for Tier 3 Electric Motors and High-Efficiency Generators in Outbound. Here is how to find and craft them.

1. Location: Desert Biome Caves

The primary source of raw Rare Earth Ore is in the deep caves of the Desert Biome (South-East map quadrant). Look for black crystalline clusters on the cave walls.

2. Crafting Recipe

  • Rare Earth Ore x 2
  • Copper Ingot x 1
  • Refining Station Tier 2 required

3. Quick Tip

Bring at least 2 extra batteries when cave-diving in the Desert, as the temperature lowers your van's remote charging efficiency.
