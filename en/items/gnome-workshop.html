Hidden Secret: The Gnome Workshop

Achievement: "Meet the Devs!" - Find the hidden workshop where the development team gnomes reside.

1. Map Coordinates

The workshop is hidden in the South-East Desert Quadrant. Look for a small rock crack near the large cactus shaped like a hand. (Coordinates: X: 442, Y: -891 approx.)

2. How to Enter

You need a Mini-Drone (Tier 2 Crafting) to fly through the crack first and hit a hidden lever. Once the lever is hit, the rock wall will open for your character to walk in.

3. Rewards

  • Achievement: "Meet the Devs!"
  • Collectible: Developer's Cup (Van decoration)
  • Secret Lore notes about the world of Outbound.
