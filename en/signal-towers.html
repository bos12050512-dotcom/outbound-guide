🔧 Signal Tower Unlocking Order

Stop getting stuck on "mushroom towers"! Complete roadmap to unlock all signal towers in the correct order.

Updated for Patch 1.0.6 | Last verified: May 20, 2026

⚠️ Common Mistake: "All towers look the same!"

Players report being stuck because all signal towers default to mushroom shape. You must unlock different blueprint variants first!

🚀 Recommended Unlock Order

1

Start at the Campfire

First, build the Wrench at your campfire. This is required before anything else.

🔓 Unlock: Basic crafting

2

Find Your First Signal Tower

Look for the tower with the green glow. This is your entry point.

📍 Location: East of your starting point

3

Unlock "Gardening" First

This is the PREREQUISITE for tower variants! Many players skip this and get stuck.

🔑 Required before: Tower variants

4

Choose Your Blueprint Wisely

After Gardening, you can unlock different tower designs:

  • Mushroom (default) - Easiest to build
  • Tree - Requires 2x Redwood
  • Tower - Requires Metal Parts

❓ Common Questions

"Why do all towers look the same?"

You haven't unlocked the Gardening blueprint yet. Keep exploring and collecting scrap until you find the Gardening schematic.

"I'm stuck at 2 hours, nothing works!"

This is the most common issue. Check if you've completed the basic Wrench quest first. Without the Wrench, you can't interact with towers.

"What's the best tower type?"

It depends on your playstyle. Mushroom is easiest for beginners. Tree looks better but costs more resources. Tower provides the most range.
