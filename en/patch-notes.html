Patch 1.0.10 - Download Vouchers & Bottle Cap Wallet补丁 1.0.10 - 下载券和瓶盖钱包

May 22, 2026

NEW

🎒 Added Download Voucher & Bottle Cap 'Wallet' system!🎒 新增下载券和瓶盖"钱包"系统！

QOL

Download Vouchers and Bottle Caps do not weigh anything anymore.下载券和瓶盖不再有重量。

FIX

Overall improvement of vehicle syncing in multiplayer. Fixed vehicles slowly floating in the air.多人模式车辆同步整体改进。修复车辆缓慢漂浮在空中的问题。

FIX

Fixed an issue with building crop plots and other larger items on top of vehicles.修复在车辆顶部建造作物种植区和其他大型物品的问题。

1.0.8

Patch 1.0.8 - Balancing & Improvements补丁 1.0.8 - 平衡性与改进

May 18, 2026

BALANCE

🏃 You can now sprint at night!🏃 现在可以在夜间冲刺了！

BALANCE

Increased sprinting speed by 15%.冲刺速度提升15%。

BALANCE

Increased stamina regain (after empty bar) by 150%.体力恢复（空体力条后）提升150%。

BALANCE

Removed Homing Boots (no longer needed since you can sprint at night).移除了"归巢靴"（夜间可冲刺后不再需要）。

CRAFTING

🪵 Sawmill recipes changed: All planks now require 1 wood instead of 2 (1:1 ratio). Applies to Everwood, Silverbark, Redwood, and Blossomwood.🪵 锯木机配方更改：所有木板现在只需1个木材（1:1比例）。适用于Everwood、Silverbark、Redwood和Blossomwood。

RESOURCE

Doubled clay from deposits. Increased Everwood & Fiber in Map 1. Increased Durafiber in Map 2. Doubled Red Ore in Map 3.黏土产出翻倍。地图1增加Everwood和Fiber。地图2增加Durafiber。地图3红矿石翻倍。

QOL

Added sorting and text search to Backpack inventory. Map legend now shows counters for collected items. Map connections always visible by default.背包增加排序和搜索功能。地图图例增加收集计数器。地图间连接默认显示。

QOL

Dog companion now spawns next to you when loading a game. Fixed dog always barking when leaving vehicle.加载游戏时狗伙伴自动生成在旁边。修复离开车辆时狗一直叫的问题。

QOL

Added setting to turn off auto-centering of third-person vehicle camera. Multiplayer highlighting/pinging can now be remapped.新增关闭第三人称车辆镜头自动居中的设置。多人模式标记功能可重新绑定按键。

FIX

Fixed Cap 'n Snaps not showing on map after biome transition. Achievement Certified Researcher now includes 4th dome stations. Map 1 bridge repair reduced to 20 planks (from 30).修复生态群系转换后Cap 'n Snaps不在地图显示。认证研究员成就现在包含第四穹顶研究站。地图1桥梁修复减少到20个木板（原30个）。
