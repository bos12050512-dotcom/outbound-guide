Overview概述

Farming and cooking are core survival mechanics in Outbound. While you can scavenge food early on, establishing a sustainable food supply through farming and cooking becomes essential for long expeditions. Growing your own crops means you'll never run out of ingredients, and cooked meals provide better hunger restoration than raw materials. 农耕和烹饪是 Outbound 的核心生存机制。虽然早期可以靠收集食物，但通过农耕和烹饪建立可持续的食物供应对长途远征来说至关重要。种植自己的作物意味着永远不会缺少原料，而且烹饪的食物比原材料更好地恢复饥饿值。

The farming system lets you build planters inside or on top of your van, and cooking requires a kitchen workstation. Both systems tie into the game's crafting progression, unlocking new recipes and crop types as you advance. 农耕系统允许你在房车内部或顶部建造种植台，烹饪则需要厨房工作台。两个系统都与游戏的合成进度相关联，随着推进会解锁新食谱和作物类型。

Getting Started with Farming开始农耕

🪴

1. Build Planters1. 建造种植台

Craft planter boxes at a workbench. They can be placed inside your van or on the roof. Start with 2-3 planters for early game.在工作台合成种植箱。可放置在房车内部或屋顶。初期先建 2-3 个种植台。

🌱

2. Get Seeds2. 获取种子

Find seeds by exploring the wild, harvesting wild plants, or trading. Different biomes yield different seed types.通过探索野外、采集野生植物或交易获取种子。不同生态群系产出的种子类型不同。

💧

3. Water & Wait3. 浇水等待

Crops need water to grow. Set up a water system or water manually. Growth time varies by crop type and biome conditions.作物需要浇水才能生长。设置供水系统或手动浇水。生长时间因作物类型和生态群系条件而异。

Cooking System烹饪系统

Cooking in Outbound requires a Kitchen Workstation — one of the craftable interior modules for your van. Once built, you can combine raw ingredients (crops, foraged items, and some animal products) into prepared meals that restore significantly more hunger than eating raw ingredients. Outbound 中的烹饪需要厨房工作台 — 这是房车可合成的内部模块之一。建造后，你可以将原料（作物、采集物品和部分动物产品）组合成加工食物，恢复的饥饿值比直接吃原材料多得多。

Kitchen Workstation厨房工作台

Required for all cooking. Unlocks at early-mid crafting tier.所有烹饪都需要。在早期-中期合成等级解锁。

Ingredients食材

Raw crops, foraged items, and animal products. Check recipe requirements before cooking.生作物、采集物品和动物产品。烹饪前检查食谱要求。

Meal Quality餐食品质

Complex recipes with more ingredients yield better hunger restoration and buffs.食材越多的复杂食谱恢复饥饿值和增益越好。

Farming Tips农耕技巧

  • ✓ Rotate crops. Don't plant the same crop in the same planter repeatedly. Rotation prevents soil depletion and may yield better harvests.轮作作物。不要在同一个种植台反复种植同一种作物。轮作可防止土壤退化，可能获得更好的收成。
  • ✓ Use greenhouse planters. If available, greenhouse modules protect crops from biome weather effects and may speed up growth.使用温室种植台。如果有的话，温室模块可保护作物不受生态群系天气影响，可能加速生长。
  • ✓ Plant fast crops first. Start with berries and carrots for quick early-game food while slower crops mature.先种快熟作物。先种浆果和胡萝卜获取快速早期食物，同时等待慢速作物成熟。
  • ✓ Plan for long trips. Harvest all ripe crops before heading to a new biome. Cook meals in bulk and store them for the journey.为长途旅行做准备。前往新群系前收获所有成熟作物。批量烹饪餐食并储存以备旅途所需。
  • ✓ Keep spare seeds. Always save at least 2-3 seeds of each crop type. Losing a crop to bad weather without backup seeds is a setback.保留备用种子。每种作物至少保留 2-3 颗种子。没有备用种子时因恶劣天气失去作物会很麻烦。
