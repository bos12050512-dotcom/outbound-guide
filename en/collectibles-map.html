Collectible Types

USB Keys (15 total)

Scattered data drives containing lore audio logs and secret blueprints. Collect all 15 for the "Full Signal" achievement.

Cairns / Stone Stacks (12 total)

Ancient rock formations left by previous travelers. Interacting with all 12 reveals a hidden map fragment and unlocks the "Pathfinder" achievement.

Hidden Areas (8 total)

Secret locations not marked on the main map. They contain rare resources, unique crafting materials, and lore fragments.
