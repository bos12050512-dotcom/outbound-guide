Recommended Unlock Order

  1. Storage Module S — fewest trips back to base.
  2. Solar Panel T1 — stable energy without weather risk.
  3. Cooking Station — convert raw food to longer-lasting meals.
  4. Battery Cell — buffer power for night and bad weather.
  5. Planter Box — long-term renewable food.
  6. Wind Turbine T1 — secondary power source for storms / night.
