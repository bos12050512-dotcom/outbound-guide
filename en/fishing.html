🚀 How to Start Fishing🚀 如何开始钓鱼

Getting started with fishing in Outbound is simple. Follow these steps to begin your fish-catching journey:在Outbound中开始钓鱼非常简单。按照以下步骤开始你的捕鱼之旅：

1
Find a Water Spot寻找水域
Look for fishing spots near lakes, rivers, and coastlines in any of the 4 biomes. Interactive fishing spots will appear when you get close.在4个生态群系中寻找湖泊、河流和海岸线附近的钓鱼点。靠近时会出现可交互的钓鱼点。
2
Cast Your Line抛出鱼线
Interact with the fishing spot to cast your line. A casting animation will play, and your bobber will land in the water.与钓鱼点交互以抛出鱼线。播放抛竿动画后，浮漂会落在水面上。
3
Wait for a Bite等待鱼咬钩
Watch your bobber closely. When a fish bites, you'll get a visual and audio cue. React quickly to reel it in!密切注意浮漂。当鱼咬钩时，会出现视觉和音频提示。快速反应收线！
4
Check Your Journal查看日志
Caught fish are automatically added to your journal. Open it anytime to review your collection and see which species you're still missing.捕获的鱼会自动添加到你的日志中。随时打开日志查看你的收集，了解还缺少哪些物种。

💡 Tips for Catching Rare Fish💡 稀有鱼捕捉技巧

  • Explore every biome thoroughly. Rare and legendary fish are often found in remote or hard-to-reach fishing spots. Don't skip the mountain and coast biomes!彻底探索每个群系。稀有和传说级鱼类通常出现在偏远或难以到达的钓鱼点。不要跳过山脉和海岸群系！
  • Try different times of day. Some fish species only appear during certain times. Fish at dawn, midday, and dusk to maximize your chances.尝试不同的时间段。某些鱼类只在特定时间出现。在黎明、正午和黄昏时分钓鱼以最大化你的机会。
  • Revisit fishing spots regularly. Fish populations refresh over time. A spot that only gave common fish before might yield a rare catch later.定期重新访问钓鱼点。鱼类种群会随时间刷新。以前只出普通鱼的钓鱼点后来可能会产出稀有渔获。
  • Play with friends in co-op. Fishing with friends can make the grind more fun. Each player fills their own journal, so you can compare collections!和朋友联机游玩。和朋友一起钓鱼能让刷鱼过程更有趣。每位玩家填写自己的日志，所以你们可以比较各自的收集！
  • Focus on the journal. Check your fish journal regularly to see which species you're missing and which biomes still need exploration.专注于日志。定期查看鱼类日志，了解你缺少哪些物种以及哪些群系仍需探索。

❓ Frequently Asked Questions❓ 常见问题

When does the fishing update come out?钓鱼更新什么时候出？

It was released on June 23, 2026 as part of patch 1.1.4. The update is available now for all platforms.已于 2026年6月23日 随补丁1.1.4发布。更新现已面向所有平台推出。

Can you eat the fish?鱼可以吃吗？

No. Fish are purely decorative collectibles. They cannot be used as food or cooking ingredients.不可以。鱼类是纯粹的装饰收藏品。不能作为食物或烹饪材料使用。

Is fishing free?钓鱼是免费的吗？

Yes. The Fishing Update is a free update (patch 1.1.4), not a paid DLC. All players get access automatically.是的。钓鱼更新是免费更新（补丁1.1.4），不是付费DLC。所有玩家自动获得访问权限。

How many fish are there in total?总共有多少种鱼？

The exact total is still being confirmed by the community as players discover all species. So far, 15+ unique fish have been identified across the 4 biomes, with common, uncommon, rare, and legendary tiers.随着玩家发现所有物种，确切的总数仍在由社区确认中。目前已在4个群系中确认了15种以上的独特鱼类，分为普通、不常见、稀有和传说级。

Does fishing work in co-op?钓鱼支持联机吗？

Yes! Fishing is fully supported in co-op mode with up to 4 players. Each player catches fish for their own journal.支持！钓鱼完全支持最多4名玩家的联机模式。每位玩家为自己日志捕获鱼类。

Are there new achievements for fishing?有钓鱼相关的新成就吗？

Yes, the update includes fishing-related achievements tied to catching specific fish and completing your journal.是的，更新包含与捕捉特定鱼类和完成日志相关的钓鱼成就。
