📖 Fish Journal鱼类日志

Every fish you catch will be tracked in your journal, similar to how collectibles work. Complete your collection!每条捕获的鱼都会记录在你的日志中，类似于收藏品系统。完成你的收集！

  • Collection completion goal收集完成目标
  • Similar to other collectible systems类似于其他收藏品系统

Place your caught fish in fish tanks as decorative pieces for your van. Size of the fish affects the tank display!将捕获的鱼放入鱼缸作为露营车的装饰。鱼的大小会影响鱼缸的展示效果！

  • Decorative only (not food)纯装饰（非食物）

  • Customize your van with fish tanks用鱼缸自定义你的露营车

When does the fishing update come out?钓鱼更新什么时候出？

Summer 2026, as part of the 1.1 update. No exact date announced yet.2026年夏季，作为1.1更新的一部分。尚未公布确切日期。

Can you eat the fish?鱼可以吃吗？

No. The developers confirmed fish are purely decorative and will NOT be used as a food source.不可以。开发者确认鱼类纯装饰用途，不会作为食物来源。

Is fishing free?钓鱼是免费的吗？

Yes, it's part of a free update (patch 1.1), not a paid DLC.是的，这是免费更新（补丁1.1）的一部分，不是付费DLC。
