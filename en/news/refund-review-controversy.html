📅 Event Timeline📅 事件时间线

May 11, 2026

🚀 Outbound Launches🚀 Outbound发售

Game releases on Steam with 40,000+ concurrent players on day one. Initial reception is mixed with some players feeling content is shallow for the price.游戏在Steam发售，首日超过4万同时在线玩家。初期评价褒贬不一，部分玩家认为内容深度与价格不符。

May 12-13, 2026

💬 Developer Replies to Reviews💬 开发者回复评价

Square Glade Games developers begin replying to negative Steam reviews. The controversial message appears in multiple replies:Square Glade Games开发者开始回复Steam差评。争议性信息出现在多条回复中：

"If you apply for a refund, we hope you can update or delete this review" "如果您申请了退款，希望您能更新或者删除这条差评"
May 14, 2026

📰 Media Coverage Begins📰 媒体报道开始

GamesRadar+ and other gaming media pick up the story, criticizing the practice as "not a good look." The controversy spreads across gaming forums and social media.GamesRadar+等游戏媒体报道此事，批评这种做法"不太光彩"。争议在游戏论坛和社交媒体上传播。

May 14-15, 2026

📉 Streisand Effect📉 史翠珊效应

Many players who hadn't purchased the game leave negative reviews in protest. Review score drops significantly. Chinese reviews particularly affected - only 45% positive among 55 reviews.许多未购买游戏的玩家留下差评抗议。评分大幅下降。中文评价尤其受影响，55条评价中仅45%好评。
