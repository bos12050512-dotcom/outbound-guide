Coast Biome Guide海岸区域指南

Sandy beaches, ocean waves, and abundant fishing. The Coast biome is a refreshing mid-late game area with unique resources, a ferry system, and the best fishing in Outbound.沙滩、海浪和丰富的鱼类资源。海岸区域是游戏中期到后期的清新区域，拥有独特资源、渡轮系统和 Outbound 中最好的钓鱼体验。

🌊 Third Biome🌊 第三区域

📍 Overview📍 概览

The Coast is the third biome in Outbound, offering a dramatic shift from the arid Canyon. Sandy beaches stretch along the ocean, with tide pools, rocky outcrops, and a scenic lighthouse. This biome introduces ocean-based resources and boasts the best fishing opportunities in the entire game. The Coast also serves as a transportation hub — the Ferry Dock provides passage to the Mountain biome, the final area. Water is abundant here, making survival easier, but the ocean introduces new hazards like tides and strong currents near certain cliffs.海岸是 Outbound 的第三个区域，与干旱的峡谷形成鲜明对比。沙滩沿海洋延伸，有潮汐池、岩石露头和风景如画的灯塔。这个区域引入了基于海洋的资源，并拥有整个游戏中最棒的钓鱼机会。海岸还充当交通枢纽——渡轮码头提供前往最终区域山地区域的通道。这里水资源丰富，生存更容易，但海洋引入了新的危险，如潮汐和某些悬崖附近的强水流。

⛏️ Key Resources⛏️ 主要资源

🪵
Driftwood浮木

Washed up on beaches daily. A reliable, renewable wood source unique to the Coast.每天被冲上海滩。海岸特有的可靠可再生木材来源。

Used for: Coastal building, boat repairs, decorative crafting用途：沿海建筑、船只修理、装饰制作

🔮
Sea Glass海玻璃

Smooth, colorful glass fragments found on beaches. Formed by ocean tumbling over years.在海滩上发现的光滑彩色玻璃碎片。由海洋多年冲刷形成。

Used for: Decorations, light fixtures, trading with NPCs用途：装饰品、灯具、与NPC交易

🪸
Coral Fragments珊瑚碎片

Found in tide pools and shallow reef areas. Handle with care — some are fragile.在潮汐池和浅礁区域发现。小心处理——有些很脆弱。

Used for: Aquarium decorations, medicine, rare dyes用途：水族箱装饰、药品、稀有染料

🧂
Salt Crystals盐结晶

Harvested from evaporated tidal pools and rock faces near the ocean spray.从蒸发的潮汐池和海浪喷溅附近的岩石面上采集。

Used for: Food preservation, cooking recipes, water purification用途：食物保存、烹饪配方、水净化

🌊
Seaweed海藻

Grows along the shoreline and in tide pools. Plentiful and easy to gather in bulk.生长在海岸线和潮汐池中。丰富且易于大量采集。

Used for: Fertilizer, medicine, crafting adhesives用途：肥料、药品、制作粘合剂

🦐
Shellfish贝类

Found in tidal zones and shallow water. Includes clams, mussels, and oysters.在潮间带和浅水中发现。包括蛤蜊、贻贝和牡蛎。

Used for: Cooking, bait for fishing, shell crafting用途：烹饪、钓鱼饵料、贝壳制作

🗺️ Landmarks🗺️ 地标

⛴️ The Ferry Dock渡轮码头

The main transit hub between the Coast and Mountain biomes. Taking the ferry here unlocks the "Happy Ferry" achievement. The ferry operates on a schedule — check the timetable posted at the dock. Make sure you've completed your Coast exploration before boarding.海岸和山地区域之间的主要交通枢纽。在这里乘坐渡轮会解锁"开心渡轮"成就。渡轮按时刻表运行——查看码头上张贴的时刻表。确保在上船之前完成海岸探索。

🏮 The Lighthouse灯塔

A towering lighthouse on a rocky promontory. Climb to the top for a stunning ocean view and to find a hidden collectible. The light mechanism can be repaired with Copper Wire for a navigation boost.岩石岬上的一座高耸灯塔。爬到顶部可以看到令人惊叹的海景并找到隐藏的收集品。灯光机制可以用铜线修复以获得导航提升。

⛺ Beach Camp海滩营地

A small, cozy campsite set up near the shore. Features a fire pit, fishing station, and basic storage. Great as a base camp for exploring the surrounding area. Some NPCs may visit — trade with them for unique items.设在海岸附近的一个小而舒适的营地。配有火坑、钓鱼站和基础储物设施。非常适合作为探索周围区域的基地营地。有些NPC可能会来访——与他们交易获取独特物品。

🐚 Tide Pools潮汐池

Natural rock pools that fill and empty with the tide. Rich in collectible resources like Coral, Shellfish, and Sea Glass. Some rare fish can be found trapped in pools during low tide. Visit at different times for different loot.随潮汐涨落而充满和排空的天然岩池。富含珊瑚、贝类和海玻璃等可收集资源。退潮时可以在池中发现一些稀有鱼类。在不同时间到访可获得不同的战利品。

🎣 Fishing Spots🎣 钓鱼点

The Coast is the best biome for fishing in Outbound. Ocean waters offer the widest variety of fish, including several rare and legendary species. Here are the prime fishing locations:海岸是 Outbound 中最适合钓鱼的区域。海洋水域提供最广泛的鱼类种类，包括几种稀有和传奇物种。以下是最佳钓鱼地点：

Ocean Shore (North Beach)海洋海岸（北海滩）

The best general fishing spot. Cast from the sandy beach into the surf zone.最佳综合钓鱼点。从沙滩向浪花区投竿。

Common: Rainbow Trout, Sea Bass | Uncommon: Clownfish普通：虹鳟、海鲈 | 稀有：小丑鱼

Tide Pool Shallows潮汐池浅滩

Fish get trapped here during low tide. Easy catches but limited variety.退潮时鱼类被困在这里。容易捕获但种类有限。

Common: Clownfish | Uncommon: Pufferfish普通：小丑鱼 | 稀有：河豚

Old Pier旧码头

A weathered wooden pier extending into deeper water. Best for uncommon and rare catches.一座延伸到深水区的风化木码头。最适合钓稀有和罕见鱼类。

Common: Sea Bass | Uncommon: Rainbow Trout, Pufferfish普通：海鲈 | 稀有：虹鳟、河豚

Deep Ocean Drop-off深海落日崖

⚠️ Requires upgraded fishing rod. The only place to catch the Legendary Ocean Sunfish!⚠️ 需要升级的钓鱼竿。唯一可以钓到传奇大洋太阳鱼的地方！

Rare: Pufferfish | LEGENDARY: Ocean Sunfish稀有：河豚 | 传奇：大洋太阳鱼

🏆 Collectibles🏆 收集品

🧝
Garden Gnomes花园矮人

4-5 gnomes hidden near the shore, behind the lighthouse, and along rocky outcrops.4-5个矮人隐藏在海岸附近、灯塔后面和岩石露头旁。

🪨
Cairns石堆

3-4 cairns placed on beach headlands and cliffside paths. Some are partially submerged at high tide.3-4个石堆在海角和悬崖路径上。有些在高潮时部分被淹没。

🐚
Perfect Shells完美贝壳

Coast-exclusive collectible. Pristine shells hidden among beach debris. Trade for unique rewards.海岸特有的收集品。隐藏在海滩碎片中的完好贝壳。可交易获得独特奖励。

➡️ Final Biome Ahead➡️ 最终区域在前方

The Coast is the last stop before the Mountain — the final, most challenging biome. Take the ferry when you're ready for snow, rare endgame resources, and the climb to the peak.海岸是进入山地区域前的最后一站——最终也是最具挑战性的区域。准备好面对雪、稀有终局资源和攀登顶峰时乘坐渡轮。
