Signal Tower Choices (Pick-One System)信号塔选择（三选一系统）

How Signal Towers Work信号塔如何运作

At certain progression milestones, you'll activate Signal Towers that present a "pick one of three" choice. Each choice unlocks a different branch of the tech tree. You cannot undo this choice, so pick carefully!在特定进度里程碑，你会激活信号塔，出现"三选一"的选择。每个选择解锁科技树的不同分支。这个选择无法撤销，请谨慎选择！

⚠️ Many players report being stuck because they picked the wrong tower. Read this guide before choosing!⚠️ 许多玩家报告因为选错了信号塔而卡关。选择前请阅读本指南！

Common Progression Mistakes常见进阶错误

❌ Picking Mechanics First❌ 先选机械学

Storage is nice, but you can manage inventory with smart crafting. Food and power are harder to substitute.储物虽然好，但你可以通过聪明的合成来管理库存。食物和电力更难替代。

❌ Ignoring Gardening for Mushrooms❌ 忽略蘑菇需要的园艺

Many late-game recipes require cultivated mushrooms. Without Gardening, you'll be stuck buying or finding them randomly.许多后期配方需要种植蘑菇。没有园艺，你只能购买或随机找到它们。

❌ Spreading Points Too Thin❌ 点数分散太广

It's better to max one branch before starting another. Half-finished branches don't give meaningful bonuses.最好先满一个分支再开始另一个。半成品分支不会提供有意义的加成。

❌ Not Reading Tower Descriptions❌ 不看信号塔描述

Each tower clearly states what it unlocks. Many players click randomly and regret it. Always read before choosing!每个信号塔都清楚地说明了它解锁什么。许多玩家随机点击然后后悔。选择前一定要阅读！

Recommended Unlock Order推荐解锁顺序

1

Unlocks food production. Priority: plant basic crops immediately for passive food supply.解锁食物生产。优先：立即种植基础作物以获得被动食物供应。

2

Signal Tower 2 → Electronics信号塔 2 → 电子学

Unlocks power automation. Priority: battery systems for overnight energy stability.解锁电力自动化。优先：电池系统以确保夜间能源稳定。

3

Signal Tower 3 → Mechanics信号塔 3 → 机械学

Unlocks van upgrades. Priority: storage expansion, then mobility for exploring new biomes.解锁房车升级。优先：储物扩展，然后是探索新生态区的机动性。

4

Late Game → Cross-Branch Synergies后期 → 跨分支协同

Combine Gardening + Electronics for automated farms. Combine Mechanics + Electronics for smart van systems.结合园艺 + 电子学实现自动化农场。结合机械学 + 电子学实现智能房车系统。
