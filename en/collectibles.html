USB Keys / USB SticksUSB 密钥位置

USB keys unlock special content and achievements. Many players report difficulty finding specific ones like the Red Ridge Airstrip USB.USB 密钥用于解锁特殊内容和成就。许多玩家反映找不到特定位置（如红岭空降场 USB）。

HIGH DEMAND

Red Ridge Airstrip USB红岭空降场 USB

⚠️ Common Problem: Players report this USB is hidden inside a locked box near the runway.玩家报告此 USB 藏在跑道附近的锁箱内。

Location: Near the destroyed plane wreck at Red Ridge Airstrip. Look for a red toolbox on the side of the runway.位置：红岭空降场的坠毁飞机残骸附近。跑道边缘有一个红色工具箱。

📍 Red Ridge biome ✅ Found

Old Forest USB古老森林 USB

Hidden in a hollow tree stump. Look for a glowing particle effect near the forest edge.藏在空心树桩里。森林边缘附近有发光粒子效果。

📍 Old Forest biome ⏳ Unverified

Western Beach USB西部海滩 USB

Inside a washed-up crate near the lighthouse. May require swimming to reach.灯塔附近冲上岸的板条箱内。可能需要游泳到达。

📍 Western Beach biome ✅ Found

Abandoned Camp USB废弃营地 USB

Under an overturned tent. Check the tent's northeast corner.翻倒帐篷下方。检查帐篷东北角。

📍 Desert/Grassland biome ✅ Found

Radio Tower USB无线电塔 USB

At the base of the main antenna. Climb the ladder and look for a maintenance box.主天线底部。爬上梯子找维护箱。

📍 Highlands biome ⏳ Unverified

Mushroom Cave USB蘑菇洞穴 USB

Deep inside the glowing mushroom cave. Bring a light source as it's very dark.发光蘑菇洞穴深处。带光源，很暗。
