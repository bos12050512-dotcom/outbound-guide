Common Layout Mistakes常见布局错误

What to avoid when planning your van规划面包车时避免什么

🚫 Scattered Workstations分散工作站

Putting workstations far apart wastes time walking between them. Keep related stations close.把工作站放得太远会浪费时间在它们之间走动。把相关站点放近。

Tip:提示： Cluster workbench + storage + cooking in one corner将工作台+储物+烹饪集中在一个角落

🚫 Decoration Before Utility装饰先于实用

Looks great, slows progression. Focus on function first, add aesthetics later.看起来很棒，但会减慢进度。先专注于功能，之后再添加美学。

Tip:提示： Build essential modules first, decorate after day 50先构建必要模块，第50天后装饰

🚫 Heat Source Clustering热源聚集

Placing cooking + fridge nearby may cause efficiency penalty. Verify in-game.烹饪+冰箱放在一起可能会导致效率损失。在游戏中验证。

Tip:提示： Keep cooking and refrigeration on opposite sides将烹饪和冷藏放在对面两侧

🚫 No Reserved Late-Game Space没有预留后期空间

Building everything tight early means forced teardown later when you need Storage L or extra battery.早期把所有东西建得很紧凑意味着以后需要储物L或额外电池时被迫拆除。

Tip:提示： Reserve one corner for future 2×2 modules留一个角落给未来的2×2模块

Energy Management能源管理

Power your modules efficiently高效为模块供电

☀️

Solar Panels太阳能板

Primary power source. Place in direct sunlight for best efficiency.主要电源。放在阳光直射处以获得最佳效率。

🌬️

Wind Turbines风力涡轮

Supplement solar at night or cloudy days.在夜间或阴天补充太阳能。

🔋

Batteries电池

Store excess power for night use. Essential for high-energy setups.存储多余电力供夜间使用。高能耗配置必需品。

💡 Pro Energy Tip💡 能源专业提示

Calculate your total power draw before building. Workbench + Cooking + Fridge = high baseline. Add solar panels incrementally as you add modules.在建造前计算总功耗。工作台+烹饪+冰箱=高基线。随着添加模块逐步增加太阳能板。

Recommended power setup for early game:早期游戏推荐电源配置：
1太阳能板 + 1电池 = 基础可持续电源
