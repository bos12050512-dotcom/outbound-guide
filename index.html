What is Outbound? Outbound 是什么游戏？

Outbound is a cozy open-world exploration and survival-crafting game set in a utopian near future. You start with an empty electric camper van and gradually turn it into a mobile home. 《Outbound / 拾光旅人》是一款近未来乌托邦背景的舒适开放世界探索与生存建造游戏。玩家从一辆空荡荡的电动房车开始，逐步把它改造成移动之家。

The latest Fishing Update (v1.1.4) added a full fishing system, fish tanks, stairs, and doors — expanding the cozy sandbox even further. 最新的钓鱼更新（v1.1.4）新增了完整的钓鱼系统、鱼缸装饰、楼梯和门，进一步扩展了舒适沙盒体验。

This site covers everything from launch day to the Fishing Update: what to build first, how power works, all 4 biomes, fishing spots, and 100% completion. 本站覆盖从发售日到钓鱼更新的全部内容：先造什么、能源怎么供、4大群系、钓鱼点，以及100%完成攻略。

First Hour Starter Plan 首小时开荒路线

0-10 min: check power and storage 0-10 分钟：确认电力和储物

Before expanding, understand where energy is generated, where it is consumed, and how much storage space your starter van has. 扩建之前，先确认电力从哪里来、哪些设备会耗电、初始房车能放多少材料。

10-25 min: gather basic materials 10-25 分钟：采集基础材料

Prioritize wood-like, metal-like and plant resources. Keep rare components until you know the recipe path. 优先拿木材类、金属类、植物类资源。稀有零件先别乱用，等确认合成路线再消耗。

25-40 min: build a work loop 25-40 分钟：建立工作循环

Aim for a simple loop: gather resources, process materials, craft a useful module, then upgrade the van layout. 目标是形成循环：采集资源、加工材料、制作有用模块、再升级房车布局。

40-60 min: choose your direction 40-60 分钟：确定发展方向

Pick one route first: power stability, storage expansion, farming/cooking, or exploration mobility. 先选一条路线：电力稳定、储物扩容、种植烹饪，或探索机动性。

Core Systems 核心玩法系统

Energy: sun, wind and water 能源：太阳能、风能、水能

Outbound revolves around sustainable power. Energy determines how many stations, modules and comfort upgrades your van can support. Outbound 的核心是可持续供电。电力决定你能运行多少工作台、模块和舒适性升级。

Modular van building 模块化房车建造

The vehicle is your base. Space planning matters: storage, crafting stations, sleeping/comfort zones and external modules compete for limited room. 房车就是你的基地。空间规划很重要：储物、工作台、休息舒适区、外部模块都会抢有限空间。

Fishing (NEW in 1.1.4) 钓鱼（1.1.4新增）

Cast your line at water spots across all 4 biomes. Collect fish in your journal and display them in fish tanks. Each biome has unique species! 在4个群系的水域钓鱼。在日志中收集鱼类并用鱼缸展示。每个群系都有独特鱼种！

Pets and companions 宠物与伙伴

Adopt a dog at the Paws & Whiskers Lodge. Feed, pet and train your companion. The community is asking for cats next! 在 Paws & Whiskers Lodge 领养狗狗。喂养、抚摸和训练你的伙伴。社区正在呼吁加入猫咪！

FAQ

Does Outbound support Chinese? Outbound 支持中文吗？

Yes. Steam lists Simplified Chinese and Traditional Chinese among supported languages. This guide site also supports both English and Chinese. 支持。Steam 页面列出了简体中文和繁体中文。本站也支持中英双语。

What changed in Patch 1.1.4? 1.1.4补丁更新了什么？

The Fishing Update added a complete fishing system, fish tanks for decoration, stairs and doors as new buildable items. Fish can be caught across all 4 biomes and tracked in a journal. 钓鱼更新新增了完整的钓鱼系统、鱼缸装饰、楼梯和门等新建造物品。所有4个群系都可以钓鱼并在日志中追踪。

Can I play solo? 可以单人玩吗？

Yes. The game can be played alone or together with up to 3 friends (4 players total). 可以。支持单人游玩，也支持最多3名好友联机（共4人）。

Is the Fishing Update free? 钓鱼更新是免费的吗？

Yes! The Fishing Update (v1.1.4) is a free content update. You just need the base game. 是的！钓鱼更新（v1.1.4）是免费内容更新，只需拥有本体游戏即可。
